Privacy Policy

How we protect and respect your personal information

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

At The Guruu Foundation, Inc., your privacy and trust are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide through our Golf Tournament website. By using this site, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, email address, mailing address, and phone number
  • Payment and billing information, including credit card details
  • Guest names and contact information for team or banquet registrations
  • Preferences related to event participation or auction use
  • Communication history and correspondence with our team

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process registrations, donations, and sponsorship payments
  • Send confirmation emails, event updates, or donor acknowledgments
  • Retain payment details (only with your permission) for use at the event (e.g., silent auction bidding)
  • Provide customer service and answer inquiries
  • Promote future events and charitable programs

3. Payment Processing and Card Storage

We utilize secure, third-party payment processors to collect and manage all financial transactions.

If you opt in during registration, we may securely retain your credit card information for use at the event (e.g., to facilitate auction bidding or bar tab settlements).

All retained payment data will be deleted from our system within 48 hours of the event's conclusion.

We do not store any financial data on our own servers.

4. Photography and Video Notice

By attending the Igniting Greatness‚Ñ¢ Golf Tournament, you acknowledge and agree that photographs and video recordings may be taken throughout the event.

These images and recordings may be used by The Guruu Foundation for:

  • Social media posts
  • Website galleries
  • Future promotional materials
  • Press releases and fundraising content

We do not sell or license images for commercial use. All usage is limited to nonprofit communications, community celebration, and event promotion.

If you do not wish to be included in promotional media, please inform a Foundation team member upon check-in.

5. Information Sharing

We will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited data only with:

  • Our secure payment processing provider
  • Event logistics partners (if needed for seating or meal coordination)
  • Internal volunteers or team members directly involved with tournament operations

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may update or correct your contact information at any time by emailing us at Support@TheGuruuFoundation.org
  • You can opt out of receiving promotional messages by using the "unsubscribe" link in our emails
  • You may request that we delete your information from our systems (unless retention is legally required)

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encrypted payment processing, access limitations, and internal policy restrictions.

No system is completely secure, but we are committed to protecting your data with the highest level of care and compliance.

8. International Visitors

This site is intended for use by U.S. residents. International visitors are subject to local data privacy laws and are advised to review local compliance requirements.

9. Updates to this Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact:
