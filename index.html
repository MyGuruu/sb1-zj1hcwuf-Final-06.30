THE EXPERIENCE

The Guruu Foundation invites you to a day filled with generosity, momentum, and meaning. Hosted at the breathtaking Glen Annie Golf Club in Santa Barbara, CA, this tournament brings together friends, visionaries, and community champions for a celebration of purpose and play.
