Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining us for this incredible day.

What's included in my registration?

+

Each package includes different levels of tournament access, meals, gifts, and banquet experiences. Check the detailed breakdown for your selected package above.

Can I register multiple people under one package?

+

The Corporate Team package includes multiple registrations. For additional individual registrations, please select multiple Single Player or Super Guruu packages.

What if I can't play golf but want to support the cause?

+

Perfect! Our Banquet Only option lets you be a part of the evening celebration, enjoy great food, and connect with our community‚Äîno golf required.

You can also support The Guruu Foundation by:

  • Making a direct donation
  • Sponsoring the entry fees for a young entrepreneur or emerging leader
  • Donating raffle prizes or silent auction items

Every contribution helps us ignite greatness and expand access to mentorship.

Can I bring someone who isn't playing to the banquet?

+

Yes! You can reserve Banquet Only tickets during registration. It's a great way for friends, family, or colleagues to join the celebration‚Äîeven if they're not swinging a club.

And for Qualified Mentors in your life, you may bring that individual as your guest, courtesy of MyGuruu.

What happens if it rains?

+

Although rain is highly unlikely in September, the tournament is rain or shine! If weather conditions pose a safety risk, we'll adjust the schedule or pivot the day's activities, to ensure everyone still enjoys a meaningful experience.

How can I get involved beyond playing or attending?

+

We highly encourage and welcome volunteers, donors, and community champions. You can support the event in a number of ways before, during and after the event.

Reach out to our Volunteer Coordinator at Hello@TheGuruuFoundation.org. Every contribution creates a memorable impact.

Are donations tax-deductible?

+

Yes! The Guruu Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 33-4378647). All contributions are tax-deductible as allowed by law.

Can I pay by check or offline?

+

Yes. If you'd like to pay offline or by check, please contact us directly at Hello@TheGuruuFoundation.org and we'll guide you through the process.

What's the refund policy?

+

We understand plans can change. Please contact us at TheGuruuFoundation@GMail.com for refund requests. We'll work with you to find the best solution.

Can I upgrade my package after registering?

+

Absolutely! Contact us and we'll help you upgrade to a higher-tier package. You'll only pay the difference in price.

What should I wear to the tournament and banquet?

+

Casual golf attire is perfect for the course‚Äîcollared shirts, golf shoes, and no denim. The banquet is relaxed, welcoming, and doesn't require formal dress.

Is my company donation or sponsorship tax-deductible?

+

Absolutely. The Guruu Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations and sponsorships are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law.

Feeling stuck in the trap?

Our team is here to help you select the perfect registration package and answer any questions about the tournament.
